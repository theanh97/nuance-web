**The Problem:** Digital notes are static images. They lose the "thought process" - the hesitation, the speed, the order of ideas.
**The Breakthrough:** A lightweight "Living Player" embed for Obsidian/Notion.
*   **Mechanism:** Instead of an image, embed a tiny script that "re-draws" the note stroke-by-stroke when the user lingers on it.
*   **Data Strategy:** Stores vector data (JSON) instead of pixels. This is crucial training data for AI to understand *how* humans think, not just *what* they write.
*   **Value:** "Knowledge is a verb." See the thought unfold.
